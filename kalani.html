Kalani Surf School

In collaboration with Kalani Surf School, I had the opportunity to design their distinctive logo and create an eye-catching T-shirt design. Drawing inspiration from 70's surf brands and the couple's passion for teaching kids, the logo encapsulates the spirit of Kalani Surf School with its dynamic imagery and fluid typography. The T-shirt design I crafted serves as a wearable canvas, combining endearing illustrations and a laid-back vibe that effortlessly reflects the essence of the school's Hawaiian surfing experience.
